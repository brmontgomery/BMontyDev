About Me

In 2014, I stumbled across the world of programming and immediately fell in love. I had the power to create whole worlds at my fingertips and found joy in solving puzzles and reaching the goals I put forth for myself.

Fast forward to 2024 and my passion is now my career. I've taught kids to code, create bespoke software solutions, and use those same skills to support IT operations in the US, UK, and Japan.

I've learned that I am at my happiest when able to solve real problems for real people, and see how my contributions create a positive impact on their work and life. However, I also enjoy learning and exploring new aspects of the tech space, whether that means firing up a home-built graphics engine to explore a new sorting algorithm or finding a new programming language to play and build with.

When I'm not tinkering with tech on my computer, you might find me playing a factory builder or tactical game (I just can't stop finding problems to solve apparently), supporting my running-fanatic wife from the safety of my bike, or taking in the beauty of the local mountains with my dogs.
